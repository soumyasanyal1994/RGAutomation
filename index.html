About Us

Who We Are

RG AUTOMATION PRIVATE LIMITED is a single window solution house for Automation, Electrical & Instrumentation product and few services. We have our Headquarter at Kolkata. Our presence in almost all the major cities in Eastern region. With a team of highly trained and experienced business experts with more than 25 years' experience in the same field RG AUTOMATION began its operations in the Automation sector and continuously diversifying into the INDUSTRIAL Sector, SOLAR as well as PROCESS industry.

Our aim is to give Quality Solutions with high level of satisfaction to our customer and make them comfortable for purchasing on regular basis. We offer a large Varity of products starting form PLC, HMI, Variable Frequency Drive (VFD), Sensor, Instruments, Switch Gear, Solar products etc. with world class manufacturer.
